The problem

Check-in is where private revenue is won or lost

Irish private hospitals still run reception on paper forms and manual PAS entry. Every missed policy number and every retyped record slows the journey from treatment to insurer payment — while your front-desk team spends the day typing instead of helping patients.

Front desk

Manual entry, queues, errors

Receptionists retype patient details from paper forms into the PAS. Queues build at clinic peaks, staff are stretched, and small errors ripple into billing.

Insurance capture

Missed or wrong policy details

Insurer, plan and membership numbers captured incorrectly at check-in mean claims stall later — and private income leaks that's never recovered.

Cash flow

Claims wait, payment waits

Incomplete registration data delays claim preparation and consultant sign-off. Every extra week a claim sits unsubmitted is working capital tied up.

How it works

From arrival to registered in one conversation

A patient walks up, speaks or taps, and Eirim Welcome does the rest — no app download, no account, no queue.

Greet & identify

The kiosk welcomes the patient by voice or touch, finds their appointment, and confirms their details — in plain conversation, with full accessibility support for older patients.

VOICE + TOUCH · MULTILINGUAL

Verify insurance

Eirim captures and validates the patient's insurer, plan and membership number — VHI, Laya or Irish Life Health — plus medical card details where relevant. Nothing missed, nothing mistyped.

POLICY OCR + VALIDATION

Register to your PAS

A clean HL7 ADT message registers or updates the patient directly in your existing PAS — no retyping, no swivel-chair work. Ready for the national EHR when it arrives.
